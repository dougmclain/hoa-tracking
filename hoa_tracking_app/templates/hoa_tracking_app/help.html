Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new association, navigate to the "Create Association" page from the main menu or from your dashboard. Fill out the required information about your homeowners association and click "Create Association". Once created, you can start adding properties, owners, and other information.

After creating an association, go to the "Properties" section and click "Add Property". You can add properties one at a time or use the bulk import feature to add multiple properties at once using a CSV file.

Use the "Payments" section to record dues, payments, and financial transactions. You can set up recurring dues schedules, track payment statuses, and generate financial reports to keep your association's finances organized.
